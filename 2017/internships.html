Scolarships from EDUCA

The doctoral school of the University of Cadiz, EDUCA, covers 50% of the registration fees of the school for the first 15 PhD students of the University of Cádiz.

Intership at Altran

Our collaborator Altran kindly offers one full time intership for the students attending SC-Camp 2017 to join them during six months (from January 1st 2018 to June 30th 2018) in their R&D&I offices in Cádiz through Altran’s BK2 program. A competition will be organized by Altran and the internship will be offered to the winner.
