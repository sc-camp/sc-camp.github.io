Application & Registration

Important dates

Applications are closed!

  • Application deadline: 22nd of September 30th of September
  • Acceptance notification: 1 week after the candidate application

Cost

The cost for the participation to the SuperComputing Camp 2017 is 300 euros per student.

The registration fee includes lunches and access to all scientific lectures, practical sessions and keynotes.

Accommodation is not included but suggestions are available on the information page

Application procedure

The number of participants is limited. Please register as soon as possible!

1. Submit your application

Submit your application via the online form. You will immediately receive an email confirming your application.

2. Notification of acceptance

We will review your application and we will send you your notification of acceptance within a week.

3. Payment & Registration

We will communicate you the payment procedure: only bank transfer is accepted, no credit card.

Please proceed to the payment as soon as possible. Your place for SC-Camp 2017 can only be guaranteed upon reception of your payment.

For any questions, you can contact us at sc2017 (a) sc-camp.org.
