Fees

Registration fees include school classes and subsistence fees during the week of study and the Gala dinner.

  • Lecturers, researchers, PhD and Master students: $100 USD
  • Other participants:
    • $130 USD if registering before 10/06/2018
    • $150 USD if registering after 10/06/2018
  • Travel fee (optional): $25 USD

All fees should be paid before 02/07/2018.
