About Colima Mexico


Safety tip: Money

The currency of Mexico is Peso.

Visitors are able to exchange their money or use their international credit card without any issue. Nevertheless, we strongly recommend to bring US dollar to easy the exchange process upon arrival.


Immigration requirements

Citezens from some countries must apply for a visa to come to Mexico. Please contact the Mexican consulate on your country to get updated information of visa constraints.
