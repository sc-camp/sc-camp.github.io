Application & Registration

Applications are open!

Important dates

  • Application opening: 24th of November 2020
  • Application deadline: 27th of November 2020
  • Acceptance notification: 28th of November 2020

Fees

The cost for the participation to the SuperComputing Camp 2020 is assumed by RENATA. The registration fee includes access to all scientific lectures, practical sessions and keynotes.

Application procedure

The number of participants is limited to 50. Please register as soon as possible!

1. Submit your application

Submit your application via the online form. You will immediately receive an email confirming your application.

2. Notification of acceptance

We will review your application and we will send you your notification of acceptance by 28th November.

3. Payment & Registration

We will communicate you the payment procedure after your acceptance.

For any questions, you can contact us:

  • by email: academico@renata.edu.co
  • or visit the sites : https://rb.gy/efbswv or www.renata.edu.co
